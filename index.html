Reaching Bikers and their families since 1998

Curtis Hubbell and his wife Teresa have been following God's call on their lives to reach Bikers and their families in a variety of ways.

HonorBound Motorcycle Ministry International

HonorBound Motorcycle Ministry is the Assemblies of God's National Motorcycle Ministry. Curtis has been serving as HonorBound National leadership for over a decade, and is now the first to take HonorBound outside the US to Ukraine.

Run For The Wall

Curtis served as a chaplain on Run For The Wall for over a decade providing emotional support for veterans of foreign wars as they travel on this difficult journey of healing across the nation.

Kidz Zone

Kidz Zone is where it all started. Early on, it was just Curtis and his family travelling to motorcycle events across the nation with a suitcase holding some magic tricks and puppets. Through the years this grew to an operation with multiple teams hosting events for hundreds of children, with the largest event attended being Sturgis Bike Week in South Dakota.

Steel City Biker Church

Steel City Biker Church operated for several years as a ministry to Bikers in Pueblo, Colorado. Hosted on Thursdays, the church offered more casual environment appealing to bikers in the community and assisted in many charity events the Pueblo biker community was involved in.
